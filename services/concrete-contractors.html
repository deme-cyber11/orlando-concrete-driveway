Our Concrete Contractor Process

We've refined our process over years of experience to ensure smooth, stress-free projects with outstanding results:

1

Free Consultation & Site Assessment

We start with a thorough on-site evaluation of your project. Our concrete contractors assess soil conditions, drainage requirements, access points, and any existing concrete that needs removal. This comprehensive assessment ensures accurate planning and pricing.

2

Detailed Written Quote

Within 24-48 hours, you'll receive a comprehensive written estimate detailing all costs, materials, timeline, and project specifications. No hidden fees, no surprises - just clear, honest pricing.

3

Scheduling & Preparation

Once you approve the quote, we schedule your project and handle all necessary permits. We'll coordinate delivery of materials and prepare the site, including removal of old concrete if needed.

4

Site Work & Base Preparation

Proper preparation is critical for long-lasting concrete. We excavate to the correct depth, compact the subgrade, install a gravel base, and set up forms with precision. For driveways, we install wire mesh or rebar reinforcement.

5

Concrete Pouring & Finishing

We pour fresh, high-quality concrete and work it to the correct thickness. Our experienced finishers create smooth, even surfaces with proper slope for drainage. Control joints are cut to prevent random cracking.

6

Curing & Protection

Proper curing is essential for concrete strength. We apply curing compound and provide barriers to protect the fresh concrete. You'll receive detailed care instructions for the curing period.

7

Final Walkthrough & Warranty

After curing, we conduct a final walkthrough to ensure your complete satisfaction. We provide warranty documentation and maintenance tips to keep your concrete looking great for decades.

Frequently Asked Questions About Concrete Contractors

How do I choose the right concrete contractor in Orlando?

Look for licensed and insured contractors with proven experience, positive reviews, and a portfolio of completed projects. Always get multiple quotes, ask for references, and ensure they offer written warranties on their work. Avoid contractors who demand large upfront payments or won't provide written estimates.

How much do concrete contractors in Orlando charge?

Concrete contractor rates in Orlando typically range from $6-$18 per square foot depending on the project type. Standard driveways cost $6-$10/sq ft, while stamped or decorative concrete ranges from $12-$18/sq ft. Factors affecting price include project size, site preparation needs, concrete thickness, and finish type. We provide free, detailed estimates for all projects.

Are your concrete contractors licensed and insured?

Yes, Orlando Concrete Driveway Experts is fully licensed and insured in the state of Florida. We carry comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage to protect both our team and your property. We're happy to provide proof of insurance upon request.

How long does a typical concrete project take?

Most residential concrete projects take 1-3 days for installation, plus 7 days for initial curing before light use (walking) and 28 days for full cure before heavy loads (vehicles). A standard driveway typically takes 2 days, while larger projects like pool decks may take 3-5 days. We provide accurate timelines with every quote.

Do you offer warranties on your concrete work?

Yes, we offer a comprehensive 5-year warranty on workmanship and a 1-year warranty on materials. Our warranty covers structural defects, proper drainage, and installation quality. We provide detailed warranty documentation upon project completion.

What's the best time of year for concrete work in Orlando?

Concrete can be poured year-round in Central Florida, though spring and fall offer the most ideal conditions. We adjust our techniques for hot summer days and cooler winter mornings to ensure proper curing. Rain can delay projects, so we monitor weather forecasts carefully and communicate any schedule adjustments.

Can you match existing concrete?

While exact color matching with existing aged concrete is challenging, we can get very close using specialized coloring techniques. We'll assess your existing concrete and recommend the best approach for seamless integration or intentional contrast designs.
